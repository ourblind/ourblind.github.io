Resources

This is a non-exhaustive list of resources the OurBlind community has found helpful. It may grow in time and suggestions are appreciated.

  • Accessibility Support Phone Lines You Should Know

  • NFB free slate and stylus program

  • NFB free white cane program

  • Braille learning resources:

    • UEB Online
    • APH UEB Math Tutorial
    • APH Nemeth Tutorial

  • Share Braille - A platform for requesting completely free secondhand braille books from other individuals to keep forever and listing your own that you don’t need anymore. Mostly kids books but definitely some adult books, with some grade one available. Not all are UEB so be sure to check the book details before requesting.

  • Deque University Scholarships - Web-based training on web and document accessibility, provided for free by a well-known industry player.
